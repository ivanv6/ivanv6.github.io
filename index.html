Biography

Li lingues differe solmen in li grammatica, li pronunciation e li plu commun vocabules. Omnicos directe al desirabilite de un nov lingua franca: On refusa continuar payar custosi traductores. At solmen va esser.

Computer Engineer, currently research fellow in the Network Security Group of National Research Council (IEIIT) and Ph.D student in Computer Science at University of Genoa. In 2017 he obtained his MSc Degree Cum Laude in Computer Engineering from the University of Genoa, Italy, with a thesis entitled "Study of security issues on ZigBee networks in reference to the Internet of Things phenomenon". For this work, he received a degree award for the "Best project 2017" for IoT networks implementation offered by the multinational company ABB.

Research interest

My research interest and topics are:

  • Network security
  • Internet of Things
  • Internet of Things security
  • Development of innovative cyber-threats
  • Machine learning for anomaly detection of cyber threats
